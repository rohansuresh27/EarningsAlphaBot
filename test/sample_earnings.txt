Q4 2024 Earnings Call Transcript

CEO Opening Statement:
I'm pleased to report that our revenue growth exceeded expectations, reaching $500 million this quarter, representing a 15% year-over-year increase. Our profit margins improved to 28%, driven by operational efficiencies and strategic cost management.

Looking ahead, we maintain our positive outlook for FY2025. We forecast continued growth in our core markets, with guidance suggesting revenue growth of 12-15% and margin expansion of 100-150 basis points.

CFO Statement:
Our performance this quarter demonstrates strong execution across all business segments. The decrease in operating expenses by 5% while maintaining growth shows our commitment to profitable scaling. Our strategy to focus on high-margin products has proven successful.
